I made a programming language and heres how you can help

Hi, I'm StuffzEZ and I am a developer. I think everyone should know at least a bit of code, in any language so I decided to make Sifzz.

Sifzz

Sifzz is a programming language developed by me, inspired by Skript's (the minecraft plugin) coding language. It is simple, like plain english. For example, look at this:

create list fruits
add "apple" to fruits
add "banana" to fruits
add "orange" to fruits

for each fruit in fruits:
    say fruit
end for

That is an example of a for loop that says the name of each fruit in fruits. Easily readable by people fluent in English. And thats one of the things I am going to talk to you about today...

Sifzz Needs Your Help

Weather you're a coder, translator or just want to test it- you can help with Sifzz. Here are the ways you can help:

Modules

Modules are a core part of Sifzz, making things JSON parsing and file reading/writing.

Fixing Bugs

Do you know python? That means you can help with fixing bugs to make Sifzz work!

Testing

Nothing to do? Want to learn Sifzz? Why not test it at the same time? We need testers to report bugs to us so that we are aware of them and can fix them!

Spreading the word

None of this above looks like anything you want to do? Why not just tell people about it! This could then get more people to do the things above! The most helpful thing would be to share this blog post.

And so much more...

Why should I help?

Aswell as Sifzz being a good language for beginners, it would also be a good language for education and your small acts today, such as testing and reporting bugs could help inspire a future generation of coders!
