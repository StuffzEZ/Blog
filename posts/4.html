Hi, im StuffzEZ and this guide will go over QUIZITAL | A Modern Quiz App For The Web.

Quizital is a HTML, CSS and JS open source quiz platform made by me (under the Quizital organisation on github, for the url). Currently it has over 20 categories ranging from Space to Harry Potter to even The Price is Right!Quizital works on almost any device with Global multiplayer coming soon. It is also going to be coming out with a schools feature so that there are different topics on things like History and Geography, not just "General Geography" or "General History".

The Current Features Of Quizital Are:

  • Local Multiplayer- Perfect for events where everyone is at the same place
  • Single Player- Practice your knowledge by yourself
  • QuickPlay- A Quick, Easy Simple Dashboard To Use To Quickly Play Your Favourite Quizzes
  • Question Limit- Don't want to play all of the questions in your category? Just set your preferred maximum question limit!
  • FullView (Beta)- View all of the topics and all of the subtopics! Perfect for choosing precise categories
  • Leaderboard- See how much points you have!
  • Large display mode- On a big screen? No worries; Quizital can scale it up for you in play mode!
  • Volume controls- Control the volume of sound effects like the correct sound, incorrect sound and clock ticking sound
  • Easy to use controls
  • End quiz button- Too late and need to end the quiz quickly? Use this button!
  • Sound effects- Know things like when you got a question correct, incorrect or when a second has gone by of your time, without looking at the screen!
    And much more to come..
